What Happens If Calories Are Not Burned?

Excess calories don't vanish — they transform inside your body

⚡
  • Extra calories get stored as body fat — especially belly fat
  • Gradual weight gain over weeks and months
  • Increased risk of Type 2 Diabetes and high blood pressure
  • Low energy levels, fatigue, and poor concentration
  • Weakened immune system and poor sleep quality
  • Higher risk of heart disease and joint pain

Fitness Tips for Andhra Lifestyle

Simple habits that make a big difference

🌅
Morning Walk is Magic
A 30-minute morning walk burns 150 kcal and boosts your metabolism for the entire day. Try walking to the nearby temple or market.
🍚
Control Rice Portions
One cup of rice = 240 kcal. Swap half your rice with more sambar and vegetables to feel full with fewer calories.
💧
Drink Water Before Meals
Drinking 500ml water 30 minutes before meals can reduce calorie intake by 13%. It fills your stomach naturally.
🧘
Evening Yoga / Stretching
15 minutes of yoga or stretching after dinner improves digestion, reduces stress, and burns 60–80 kcal.
🌶️
Andhra Spices Help!
Turmeric, pepper, and ginger in Andhra cooking have metabolism-boosting properties. Your food already has hidden fitness benefits!
🥗
Add Raw Salads
Add cucumber, onion, and tomato salad to every meal. It's filling, low in calories (30 kcal) and packed with fibre and vitamins.
